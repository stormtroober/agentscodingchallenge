# Billing Policy

## Subscription Plans

### Basic Plan - $9.99/month
- 5 projects
- 10 GB storage
- Email support (48-hour response)
- Community access

### Professional Plan - $29.99/month
- Unlimited projects
- 100 GB storage
- Priority support (24-hour response)
- API access
- Advanced analytics

### Enterprise Plan - $99.99/month
- Unlimited everything
- Dedicated support (4-hour response)
- Custom integrations
- 99.9% SLA
- Dedicated account manager

## Billing Cycle

- Monthly plans are billed on the same day each month
- Annual plans receive a 20% discount
- Charges processed automatically via stored payment method
- Invoices sent via email within 24 hours of charge

## Refund Policy

### Full Refund Eligibility
- **Monthly plans**: Within 14 days of initial purchase or renewal
- **Annual plans**: Within 30 days of initial purchase or renewal
- Full refund processed to original payment method

### Partial Refunds
- Available when downgrading plans mid-cycle
- Calculated as: (Days remaining / Total days) × Price difference
- Applied as account credit by default
- Can request direct refund to payment method

### Non-Refundable
- Usage after 14/30 day window
- Add-on features used during the billing period
- Enterprise custom development work

## Payment Methods

### Accepted Payment Methods
We accept the following payment methods for all plans:

| Method | Processing Time (Refunds) |
|--------|--------------------------|
| Credit/Debit Card | 5-10 business days |
| PayPal | 3-5 business days |
| Bank Transfer (Enterprise only) | 7-14 business days |

### By Plan Type
- Basic/Professional: Standard processing
- Enterprise: Expedited processing (2-3 business days)

## Cancellation

### Process
1. Go to Settings → Subscription → Cancel
2. Confirm cancellation reason
3. Service continues until end of current billing period
4. No charge for next billing period

### Data Retention
- Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation
- Export your data before canceling
- After 30 days, data is permanently deleted

## Disputes

If you believe a charge is incorrect:
1. Contact billing support within 30 days
2. Provide: transaction ID, date, amount, reason
3. We'll investigate within 5 business days
4. Resolution communicated via email

## Payment Failures

### Automatic Retry
- Failed payments retry automatically after 3 days
- Second retry after 7 days
- Account suspended after 14 days of failed payments

### Resolution
1. Update payment method in Settings → Billing
2. Click "Retry Payment"
3. Service restored immediately upon successful payment

## Taxes

- Prices shown exclude applicable taxes
- VAT/GST applied based on your location
- Tax invoices available in dashboard
- Tax-exempt? Contact support with documentation
